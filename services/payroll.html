Payroll processing services for businesses

Need help with payroll processing for your business? Our payroll professionals will take care all the payroll tasks right from collecting the timesheets to sending on time payments. Just let our professional handle the payroll and you can focus on your business.

How does payroll outsourcing work?

  • You send us all relevant information timesheets, overtime, software logs etc.
  • Our payroll agents will prepare all the necessary payroll information
  • If there is any missing information we will contact you and get them
  • Upon receipt of all information we will complete your payroll and process it

What payroll tasks can we do for you

  • Weekly, bi-weekly, monthly payroll
  • Maintain vacation and sick leave calculation
  • Accurately calculate overtime
  • Prepare quarterly and yearly IRS forms (940 and 941)
  • Prepare W-2 and 1099

Some of your benefits

  • You get more time to focus on your growing your core accounting business
  • You don't have to worry about recruiting payroll staff or managing them
  • Your payroll will be completed well before payday
